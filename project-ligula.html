Approach

Orchestrated setup journeys that meet teams where they are

We shadowed customer success calls and discovery sessions to map the core jobs-to-be-done. Those insights shaped a modular onboarding flow that adapts based on company size and integrations selected.

Lifecycle messaging aligns with each milestone, nudging admins with personalized coaching rather than generic tips.

Key workstreams
