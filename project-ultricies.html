Approach

Unlocking signal across fragmented data sources

Legacy EHR integrations slowed down clinicians and obscured critical patient data. We partnered with Medisight to build a normalized data layer, facilitating real-time updates for every specialty without compromising compliance.

By aligning with medical directors, we mapped a guided workflow that promoted key patient context while remaining flexible for varying specialties and device form factors.

Solution highlights

  • Contextual patient summary tile with lab trending and watchlists.
  • Role-based access scoped via Okta and AzureAD for rapid hospital onboarding.
  • Offline-ready mobile workflows syncing through FHIR subscriptions.

Interface details

Designed to delight clinicians, not distract.

Care team dashboard

Mission control hub for rounding physicians with prioritized cohorts, escalations, and a unified to-do queue.

Predictive alerting

Explainable AI surfaces risk with supporting factors, enabling faster escalations and confident decisions.

Patient timeline

Time-based visualization of vitals, medications, and interventions that scales down to tablet form factors used on the move.

Outcomes

Making every minute count in patient care

Clinical staff gained the visibility needed to intervene earlier. Automated audit logs and double-entry prevention removed thousands of manual steps per month.
