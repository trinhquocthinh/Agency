Solution

Composable services for speed and flexibility

We aligned stakeholders on a roadmap that separated experience, commerce engine, and content delivery, enabling teams to ship improvements independently. Localization frameworks handled currency, fulfillment, and regulatory nuance per region.

  • Unified product catalog with localized pricing feeds.
  • Custom checkout flows with PSD2 compliant SCA and fraud prevention.
  • CMS-driven merchandising blocks that marketing can update without deploys.

Timeline
