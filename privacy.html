Your data, respected

How Adex handles information.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, analytics derived from product usage, and limited third-party data to enrich our services.

2. How we use data

Data powers tailored experiences, supports customer success, and keeps our systems secure. We never sell personal information.

3. Sharing & disclosure

We share data only with vendors who help us deliver services, under strict confidentiality obligations, or when required by law.

Adex applies industry-standard safeguards including encryption, access controls, and continuous monitoring to protect your data.

5. Your choices

You can update preferences, request deletion, or opt out of marketing communications any time by emailing privacy@adex.com.

6. International transfers

When transferring data outside your region we implement appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.
