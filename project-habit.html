North star

Designing for sustainable streaks

We grounded the product strategy in behavior science. Each activity within the app aligns to the Hook model—trigger, action, reward, investment—without encouraging unhealthy usage patterns.

A personalization service prioritized goal recommendations and recalibrated difficulty daily based on progress signals from connected wearables.

Personalization inputs

  • Chronotype survey
  • Wearable data
  • Calendar sync
  • Weather API
  • Voice notes

The prioritization engine blended supervised models with a rules engine to safely guide user goals.

Experience layers

Everything users need to see progress—and feel it.

Routine composer

Drag-and-drop interface that lets members build multi-step rituals with embedded inspirational content.

Social accountability

Cohort challenges and friend nudges are timeboxed to avoid burnout while keeping teammates aligned.

Progress storytelling

Weekly reflections turn health data into narrative summaries, nudging users with compassionate insights.

Lifecycle

Contextual nudges, never noise

Lifecycle experiments mapped the right channel to each moment. Push notifications became optional, replaced by adaptive in-app reminders when users preferred to self-direct.

Impact by the numbers

  • 36% decrease in churn from drop-off cohorts.
  • Net promoter score lifted by 19 points post relaunch.
  • Support tickets reduced by 42% with new in-app guidance.
